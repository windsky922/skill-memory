# Architecture And Workspace Map

This workspace currently contains graduation project materials rather than a conventional source-code repository.

## Known Areas

- `论文文献/`: literature and paper references.
- `研究数据/`: research data.
- `outputs/`: generated outputs.
- `ppt_build/`: PowerPoint build artifacts.
- `tmp/`: temporary working files.
- Root `.docx`, `.pdf`, `.pptx`, `.zip`: thesis, reports, support documents, and defense materials.

## Working Principles

- Prefer editing final deliverables in a controlled output path.
- Keep temporary analysis under `tmp/` or a task-specific output directory.
- Do not overwrite source documents unless explicitly requested.
- For repeatable analysis, prefer notebooks or scripts over manual one-off transformations.
